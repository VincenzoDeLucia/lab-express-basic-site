Since a very young age, Vincenzo knew web development would be his path in life. That's why he spent most of it trying to become a biologist, a music producer, a game designer, a journalist and a professional internet troll. After having reached the pinnacle in each of these, he felt like it was time for him to make his mark in the world of web development: this website is the one of the first steps our hero took towards greatness.
